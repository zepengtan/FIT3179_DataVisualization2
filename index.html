ABORTION IN USA

A comparitive study of abortion cases in the United States of America for different age groups from the year 1983 - 2017.

Abortion Cases in Each State, 2017

Detailed Abortion Cases from 1983 - 2017

The sudden decrease in cases in 1988, 1992, 1996, and 2000 might be due to missing data. However, it is clear to see that abortion cases have dropped rapidly since 2005. According to studies, this might be due to birth and pregnancy declines.

Women aged 20 - 34 contribute the most toward abortion cases. This is logical as this is the period when young adults and adults are mature. They might not want to settle down and form a family, thus deciding to perform abortions.

Detailed Abortion Cases in Each State, 2017

The South has more total abortion cases in 2017, followed by the Northeast. These are New York, Maryland, District of Columbia, and New Jersey. Similarly, women aged 20 - 34 contribute the most in these states.

However, an interesting observation is that the rate of cases for women aged 35 - 39 is higher compared to other states. Thus, this might lead to an overall high abortion case rate. .

Ranking of Abortion Cases Across the Year in Different States

New York and District of Columbia have consistently ranked top 5 across the years. However, New York has ranked above Top 2 since 2000 while District of Colombia has dropped below Top 4 since 2010.

New Jersey has climbed in ranking since 2009. While California has dropped in ranking since 1988. This is also similar with Hawaii.
